Welcome to my personal webpage. I am researcher in epidemiology, health economics, and policy at the university of Zurich. I work both at the [Epidemiology, Biostatistics, and Prevention Institute](https://www.ebpi.uzh.ch/en.html/), and at the [Vokinger chair](https://www.ius.uzh.ch/en/staff/professorships/alphabetical/vokinger.html/) of the faculty of Law & Medicine.

I hold a PhD in Economics from the [University of Barcelona](https://web.ub.edu/en/home), an MSc in Health Economics and Policy from the [Barcelona School of Economics](https://bse.eu/), and a BSc in Economics from the [Pompeu Fabra University](https://www.upf.edu/).
